Easy Iced Coffee



Description

This is a 'cheaters' iced cappuccino! Very easy to make, and very good!

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons instant coffee granules
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 3 tablespoons warm water
  • 1 cup ice, or as needed
  • 6 fluid ounces cold milk

Steps:

  1. In sealable jar, combine instant coffee, sugar, and warm water.
  2. Cover the jar and shake until mixture is foamy.
  3. Pour into a glass full of ice.
  4. Fill the glass with milk.
  5. Adjust to taste if necessary.
