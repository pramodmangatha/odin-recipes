Cafe Latte



Description

This is for all fellow coffee drinkers. I love lattes but buying them can be pricey. So here's a recipe so you can make your own.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 ⅓ cups hot freshly brewed dark roast espresso coffee

Steps:

  1. Heat milk in a saucepan set over medium-low heat.
  2. Whisk briskly with a wire whisk to create foam.
  3. Brew espresso and pour into 4 cups.
  4. Pour in milk, holding back the foam with a spoon.
  5. Spoon foam over the top.
